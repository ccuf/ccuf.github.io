Mission

About Us

Cultural Centre of Uzbekistan & Friends (CCUF) is a not-for-profit cultural organization incorporated in BC, Canada. Our purpose is integrate diverse groups of immigrants and their friends from Uzbekistan. We organize variety of programs and events for members.

Services

We organize variety of programs and events. They are run by CCUF members for CCUF members. See the list of ongoing and planned programs/events below. Get in touch if any of these pique your interest, especially if you would like to volunteer to run a program.

Past Events

Gatherings are the best part of having CCUF. We occasionally meet around Vancouver, BC. The last one was on May 7, 2022 for community potluck. We have more of these planned in the near future. Please get in touch if you are interested in volunteering.
