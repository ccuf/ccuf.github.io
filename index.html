About Us

Cultural Centre of Uzbekistan & Friends (CCUF) is a not-for-profit cultural organization incorporated in BC, Canada. Our purpose is integrate diverse groups of immigrants from Uzbekistan and their friends. We do so by organizing variety of virtual and in-person events.

Events

We organize variety of programs and events. They are run by CCUF members for CCUF members. See the list of ongoing and planned programs/events here. Get in touch if any of these pique your interest, especially if you would like to volunteer to run a program.

Services

CCUF members run wide range of businesses. They include certified translation, personal real-estate, appliance services, among others. We know the business owners and recommend their service to CCUF members and the broader community.

Mission

To promote and share Uzbekistan culture, values and heritage with all communities.

Volunteer

We are always looking for volunteers. Currently available positions are event organizer, outreach member, program administrator, or custom position that you create. Please get in touch over ccuf.canada@gmail.com email.

Who are CCUF members?

You can see our leadership below. Most of CCUF members are local to BC, Canada, but we also welcome people from boarder communities. We will tell more about our members and membership rules as we evolve.
