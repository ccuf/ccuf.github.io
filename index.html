About Us

Cultural Centre of Uzbekistan & Friends (CCUF) is a not-for-profit cultural organization incorporated in BC, Canada. Our purpose is integrate diverse groups of immigrants from Uzbekistan and their friends. We do so by organizing variety of virtual and in-person events.

Services

We organize variety of programs and events. They are run by CCUF members for CCUF members. See the list of ongoing and planned programs/events below. Get in touch if any of these pique your interest, especially if you would like to volunteer to run a program.

Past Events

Gatherings are the best part of having a community. We usually meet around Vancouver, BC. The most recent one was on December 30, 2022 for community potluck. We have more of these planned in the near future. We will share photos from the past events as CCUF evolves.

Mission

To promote and share Uzbekistan culture, values and heritage with all communities.
